I am a postdoctoral scholar at Data Science Institute, University of Chicago, working with Prof. Chenhao Tan and Prof. James Evans. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Wangxuan Institute of Computer Technology, Peking University, advised by Prof. Yansong Feng in 2025. I was a visiting Ph.D. student at University of California, Los Angeles, working with Prof. Kai-Wei Chang in 2023. I received the BSc degree from School of Electronic Engineering and Computer Science, Peking University and BEc degree from National School of Development in 2020.

Research Interests

I am deeply engaged in advancing the complex reasoning abilities of language models, aiming to help humans with real-world challenges. My current research focuses on causal reasoning with LLMs and AI for Innovation.
